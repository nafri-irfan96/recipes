Ingredients:
 - Eggs(2)
 - Oil
 - Chilli Powder
 - Salt
 - Onions

 Direction(s):
 - Preheat the oil till it reaches about 150 degrees. 
 - Cut open the eggs and pour it into the boiling oil.
 - Add desired amount of chilli powder and salt to it.
 - Turn over the omlet after about 2 minutes.
 - Fry the layer until it gets cooked.
 - Serve with onions
